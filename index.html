OUR MISSION

Building a future where Zambians have pathways to strong literacy skills.

OUR COMMITMENT

Built on Four Pillars

REACHING underserved areas of Zambia with access to literacy resources.

INSPIRE to increase literacy skills through books, educational resources, and mentors with access for all learners.

SUPPORT and SUPPLY printed material and access to digital resources with the purpose of building pathways to increased literacy.

EQUIP educators, mentors, and learners with resources and training to develop ongoing literacy skills.

OUR VALUES

What We Believe

RISE believes that strong literacy skills build pathways for self motivated, strong, and responsible learners.

RISE supports the value of literacy skills that allow for curiosity, creativity, and problem solving.

RISE acknowledges the scope of literacy skills are not limited to print material, but includes reading, writing, speaking and listening.

RISE believes that reading matters. The ability to read transforms individual lives and impacts communities with the potential for positive change.

RISE is committed to the strengths of Zambian culture and the importance of Zambian leadership within the organization and on location.
